Our Philosophy

Rooted in Nature, Growing Through Play

[Draft copy — please review & edit.] At Pasadena Country Garden School, we believe children grow best like a garden — with sunlight, patience, and rich soil to root into. Nestled in the beautiful Arroyo Seco, just across from Westridge School, our little garden school offers a warm, home-like environment where curiosity leads the way.

Our days are play-based and nature-centered. Children plant, dig, harvest, and explore — building confidence, wonder, and a lifelong love of learning. Small class sizes mean every child is truly known and cared for.

What guides us

  • Garden-based learning — the outdoors is our classroom.
  • Play as the work of childhood — joyful, hands-on discovery.
  • Nourishing food — real meals made with care (Nourishing Traditions).
  • A small, loving community — where children and families belong.
